Cyclone Safety Tips

Do's Don'ts
1. Stay Informed - Monitor weather reports and warnings. 1. Don't ignore cyclone warnings.
2. Prepare an Emergency Kit - Include essentials like water, food, and first aid. 2. Don't wait until the last minute to prepare.
3. Secure Your Home - Reinforce doors and windows, and clear your yard of debris. 3. Don't leave loose objects that can become projectiles.
4. Evacuate When Advised - Follow authorities' instructions to evacuate if necessary. 4. Don't ignore evacuation orders.
5. Stay Indoors During the Storm - Avoid going outside when the cyclone is active. 5. Don't venture out during the cyclone.
6. Check on Neighbors - Help those who may need assistance, especially the elderly or disabled. 6. Don't forget to check on your neighbors and offer help.
