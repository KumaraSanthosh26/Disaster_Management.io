Landslide Do's and Don'ts

Do's Don'ts
1. Stay Informed - Monitor weather reports and warnings. 1. Don't ignore landslide warnings.
2. Create a Safety Plan - Know evacuation routes and safe places. 2. Don't build or live in high-risk landslide areas.
3. Maintain Drainage - Ensure proper drainage around your property. 3. Don't modify slopes without professional advice.
4. Watch for Warning Signs - Be alert for ground movement. 4. Don't park or camp near steep slopes.
5. Evacuate When Advised - Follow authorities' instructions. 5. Don't stay in your home if it's in a danger zone.
6. Report Landslides - Inform local authorities about any incidents. 6. Don't wait until it's too late to take action.
