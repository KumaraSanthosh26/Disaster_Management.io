Heat Wave Safety Tips

Do's Don'ts
1. Stay Hydrated - Drink plenty of water throughout the day. 1. Don't wait until you're thirsty to drink water.
2. Stay Cool - Use fans or air conditioning if available, and take cool showers or baths. 2. Don't rely on fans alone when the heat is extreme.
3. Wear Lightweight Clothing - Choose light-colored, loose-fitting clothing to stay cool. 3. Don't wear heavy or dark clothing that traps heat.
4. Limit Outdoor Activities - Avoid strenuous outdoor activities during the hottest part of the day. 4. Don't engage in vigorous exercise in extreme heat.
5. Check on Vulnerable Individuals - Look out for the elderly, young children, and those with health issues. 5. Don't forget to check on pets and provide them with water and shelter from the heat.
6. Stay Informed - Pay attention to heat advisories and warnings from local authorities. 6. Don't underestimate the risks of heat-related illnesses; seek medical help if you or someone you know shows signs of heat exhaustion or heatstroke.
