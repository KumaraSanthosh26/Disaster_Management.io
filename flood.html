Flood Safety Tips

Do's Don'ts
1. Stay Informed - Monitor weather reports and flood alerts. 1. Don't ignore flood warnings or advisories.
2. Prepare an Emergency Kit - Include essentials like water, food, flashlight, and a first aid kit. 2. Don't wait until the last minute to prepare.
3. Move to Higher Ground - Evacuate to a higher floor or elevated area if flooding is imminent. 3. Don't stay in low-lying areas or near flood-prone zones.
4. Avoid Floodwaters - Don't attempt to walk or drive through flooded streets. 4. Don't underestimate the power of moving water; it can be dangerous and unpredictable.
5. Rescue and Aid - Help others in need, especially the elderly, children, and those with disabilities. 5. Don't leave vulnerable individuals behind.
6. Stay Calm - Keep a level head and follow the instructions of emergency responders. 6. Don't panic; panic can lead to poor decision-making during a flood.
